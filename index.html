Our Goal

Our Goal is to empower startups, investors, and general users by connecting them in a decentralized and secure ecosystem at the same time providing a platform where they can network, collaborate, and access exclusive communities.

How It Works

For Investors

  • Sign up by clicking the "Get Started" button.
  • Customize your dashboard to the type of startups or businesses you want to invest in.
  • Connect your wallet by clicking the "Connect Wallet" button on your dashboard.
  • Select the startup you want to invest in.
  • Choose the token you prefer to use for your investment.
  • Approve the transaction and contribute to helping startups achieve their business goals.

For Startups

  • Sign up by clicking the "Get Started" button.
  • Create your profile and complete the ID verification process.
  • Connect your wallet by clicking the "Connect Wallet" button on your dashboard.
  • Select the startup you want to invest in.
  • Choose a business niche that aligns with your goals.
  • Submit your proposal or funding request for review.
  • Wait for approval to move forward.
